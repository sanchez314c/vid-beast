# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- **Use welcoming and inclusive language**
- **Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences**
- **Gracefully accept constructive criticism**
- **Focus on what is best for the community**
- **Show empathy towards other community members**
- **Help others learn and grow**
- **Provide constructive feedback**
- **Acknowledge and credit others' work**

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances**
- **Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks**
- **Public or private harassment**
- **Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission**
- **Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting**

## Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it through one of these channels:

1. **Email**: conduct@vidbeast.com
2. **GitHub Issues**: Create a private issue by mentioning any maintainer
3. **Discord**: Message any moderator privately
4. **Direct Message**: Contact any project maintainer directly

### What to Include

- **Description**: What happened and when
- **Individuals Involved**: Who was involved (if known)
- **Location**: Where it occurred (GitHub, Discord, etc.)
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, or other documentation
- **Impact**: How it affected you or others

### Response Process

1. **Immediate Acknowledgment**: We'll respond within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We'll investigate thoroughly and fairly
3. **Action**: We'll take appropriate action based on findings
4. **Follow-up**: We'll follow up with all parties involved
5. **Resolution**: We'll work to resolve the situation satisfactorily

## Enforcement Guidelines

### Consequences

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Possible Actions

- **Warning**: Private or public warning about inappropriate behavior
- **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community spaces
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from all community spaces
- **Content Removal**: Removal of inappropriate content
- **Report to Authorities**: Involving law enforcement if necessary

### Appeal Process

If you believe action was taken unfairly:

1. **Wait 24 hours** to cool down
2. **Email**: appeal@vidbeast.com with details
3. **Include**: Original incident, action taken, and why you disagree
4. **Response**: We'll review and respond within 72 hours

## Community Guidelines

### Communication Standards

#### GitHub Issues and Pull Requests
- **Be specific and clear** in issue descriptions
- **Provide reproduction steps** for bugs
- **Include system information** (OS, version, etc.)
- **Use constructive language** in code reviews
- **Acknowledge contributions** from others

#### Discord and Chat
- **Stay on topic** in specific channels
- **Avoid spam** or excessive messaging
- **Use appropriate channels** for different topics
- **Respect channel moderators** and their decisions

#### Code Contributions
- **Follow existing code style** and patterns
- **Write clear commit messages** explaining changes
- **Include tests** for new functionality
- **Document complex code** with comments
- **Be open to feedback** and suggestions

### Collaboration Standards

#### Code Reviews
- **Focus on the code**, not the person
- **Provide specific, actionable feedback**
- **Suggest improvements** rather than just pointing out problems
- **Acknowledge good work** and clever solutions
- **Be patient** with contributors learning the codebase

#### Design Discussions
- **Listen to different perspectives**
- **Back up opinions** with evidence or reasoning
- **Be willing to compromise** on implementation details
- **Focus on user needs** and project goals
- **Document decisions** for future reference

## Diversity and Inclusion

### Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive community where:

- **All voices are heard** and valued equally
- **Different perspectives** are welcomed and respected
- **Everyone has equal opportunity** to contribute
- **Barriers to participation** are identified and removed
- **Cultural differences** are understood and accommodated

### Inclusive Practices

- **Use inclusive language** in all communications
- **Provide multiple ways** to participate and contribute
- **Consider accessibility** in all community spaces
- **Celebrate diversity** in our community
- **Learn from mistakes** and improve continuously

## Professional Conduct

### Expected Behavior

- **Treat all community members** with respect and professionalism
- **Maintain confidentiality** when appropriate
- **Avoid conflicts of interest** in community decisions
- **Represent the project** positively and accurately
- **Follow all applicable laws** and regulations

### Prohibited Activities

- **Harassment** of any kind
- **Discrimination** based on any characteristic
- **Retaliation** against reporters of misconduct
- **Fraud or deception** in community interactions
- **Unauthorized access** to project resources
